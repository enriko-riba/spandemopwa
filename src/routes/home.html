Episode I

PWA - the battle begins

It is a period of civil war among various web technologies. The Rebel forces called Progressive Web Applications (or PWA), striking from a hidden base in SPAN d.o.o., have won their first victory against the evil Empire's native applications.

The Rebel commander Mirna Marković managed to steal secret plans to real time DB updates, materialize web components, npm and webpack workflow.

At the same time pursued by the Empire’s sinister agents, rebel Admiral Enriko Riba races to implement high level support for serviceworkers and push notification API that can save his people and restore freedom to the galaxy…

All this comes at a price. The evil Empire still prevails on the iOS system's. It is not clear how and when will the PWA forces win but everyone hopes the war will end soon...
